# Changelog

All notable changes to Hope Is Here will be documented in this file.

## [1.0.0] - 2026-03-06

### Initial Release

The first public release of Hope Is Here - your TBC adventure journal!

#### Journal System
- 7-tab journal: Timeline, Milestones, Raids, Reputation, Statistics, Armory, Social
- Automatic milestone tracking from level 1-70 with personalized stories
- Complete boss lists for all TBC raids (Karazhan through Sunwell)
- Phase-based BiS gear tracking in the Armory tab with spec-based recommendations
- Reputation tracking for all 18 TBC factions with milestone entries

#### Attunement Tracking
- Step-by-step progress tracking for Karazhan, SSC, TK, Hyjal, Black Temple, and Cipher of Damnation
- Progress bars and chapter-by-chapter quest chain visualization

#### Social Features
- Fellow Travelers system with automatic detection and colored nameplates
- RP profiles for character backstory and personality
- 20+ unlockable achievement badges
- Minimap pins for Fellow Travelers
- Activity feed notice board with reactions
- iLevel leaderboard to compare gear levels
- Guild roster tracking and member insights
- Calendar with event scheduling, raid signups, and role selection
- Built-in Soft Reserve system for raid loot

#### Crusade Critter (Mascot System)
- Tab-out housing panel with 7 unlockable mascots
- Mascot quips and reactions to boss kills, deaths, and zone changes
- Dungeon run statistics and boss kill time tracking
- Built-in boss guides for every TBC dungeon

#### Multiplayer Minigames
- Dice Roll, Rock-Paper-Scissors, Death Roll with escrow
- Tetris Battle (competitive 2-player)
- Pong, Battleship (vs AI or players), PacMan
